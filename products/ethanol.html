How it's made

The carbon dioxide (CO2) we use in our process is captured at a source point. We create hydrogen on-site that is fed into our award-winning and patented Carbon Conversion Reactor alongside the CO2 where a proprietary reaction occurs, transforming the H2 and CO2 into impurity-free ethanol, methanol and water. Our distillation process then separates the ethanol from the methanol and water.
